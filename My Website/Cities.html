Greenland

Greenland is an island nation located between the Arctic and Atlantic oceans, east of the Canadian Arctic Archipelago. This piece of land is always covered with ice throughout the year.

Lahore

Lahore is the capital of the Pakistani province of Punjab and is the country's 2nd largest city before Karachi, in addition to being the 6th largest city in the world. Lahore is one of Pakistan's wealthiest cities with an estimated GDP of $84 billion as of 2019.
